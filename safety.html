SAFETY

SAFETY

Safety responsibility. It's our standard.

At Clugs Engineering Limited, our associates adhere to strict procedures for both themselves and those working on a project site. No matter the job or the circumstances, we provide a safe working environment. That's why our Experience Modification Rate (EMR) is lower than the overall industry average.

A CULTURE OF SAFETY & RELIABILITY.

Setting the bar higher every day.

Clugs Engineering Limited safety culture and track record stem from the involvement, leadership and dedication of our management teams and associates. As a leader in the construction industry, we empower and hold all associates accountable to higher standards to enhance the safety and health of all those working on a project site. We strive to continually update our practices to provide maximum protection and performance.

A TRACK RECORD OF PERFORMANCE.

We implement and enforce safety measures through technology and training. All employees in both the field and operations partake in safety training. Our program consists of ongoing training to keep all Clugs Engineering Limited associates updated with the most accurate safety information. In addition, our safety team uses technology to provide a more efficient experience for preplanning, audits, corrective actions and continual training.
